Why this is better than DofE

For all of you who, like us, are feeling sceptical as they rememeber the cold, wet and tiring experience that was DofE, we want to show you that walking can be different. When we went walking, we had lie-ins, breaks to sit on a nice looking bridge, lots of tea, warm food and biscuits, and best of all we could go swimming after the walks. Hopefull looking through our website will convince you that there is more to walking than that time on DofE, when it was -2 degrees and then you only had an empty roll for lunch, then your friend lost their shoe in a bog and so you didn't get to the campsite till midnight and then you still had to cook that 3 course meal they insist on and so you only get 5 hours sleep. In fact walking and camping can be brilliant fun and we want to show you this.
